Project Brief: Payroll and HR Management System

A fintech company that is developing a Digital Payroll function designed for SMEs in Africa is looking for a UX designer. The company is looking for a UI/UX Challenge for Product design. The Fintech boutique offers human management systems to enhance and automate Financial services for SMEs in Africa.

Tasks:

  • UI research to understand Payroll Requirements
  • Understanding user needs
  • Creating user flow and wireframes
  • Improve interaction between users
  • Interactive Control

Product Design:

  • Understanding the Scope for each step
  • Creating wireframes to outline the basic structure and layout of the web app
  • Designing visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces
  • Testing for usability and performance
  • Providing a design manual for development guidance
